BRINGING SOLUTIONS TO YOU

BBC Restaurant: Increasing Profits

VGS helped this client, the Breakfast Brunch Cafe in Cypress, Texas to increase its profits. We carried out a data driven analysis of its revenue streams and costs to identify possible improvement areas, proposed a more effective marketing program as a possible solution, and designed a streamlined marketing campaign targeting the correct customer demographic, which resulted in more patronage and increased revenue.

In addition, we performed customer arrival time studies to better understand the peak volumes for patrons, and re-arranged employee usage to fit this, resulting in a more efficient use of staff and cost reduction.

Fusabo App: Market analysis, app marketing, app launch

VGS is currently partnering with Fusabo to help introduce this sports app to the market. From an in-depth market analysis, to product profitability analysis, to designing a marketing program for the target customer base reach, we are the 360 degree service providers to our partners.

1073 Records: Marketing and Promotion

VGS handled promotion and marketing for the music label 1073 Records and its associated acts. Online/digital marketing, radio plug ins/ads, TV ads, and print media ads/campaigns that helped break out the record label and its acts. We designed and tailored a unique marketing approach that helped reach the target fan base successfully.
