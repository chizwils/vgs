Declining profitability analysis and solution offering

We offer a top down approach to your declining profitability problem. Why is your profit declining? What revenue stream is affected? Is it tied to a general market behavior or trend? Are your costs increasing? Are you experiencing a declining volume of sales? Why? A VGS analysis encompasses all aspects of the problem. We collate all relevant data, identify the possible causes, and propose long lasting solutions through qualitative and quantitative methods.

Projects Economics Analysis

VGS offers a robust projects economics analysis over a projected time period. We collate data and calculate key economic indicators like NPV, VIR, PB, UTC, etc in order to give our clients the profit on a project over a time period, discounted to the present value of money. We also analyze and compare different project options, and advice our clients on which is a more profitable investment option.

New Product Launch Analysis

For our clients already in a market segment that desire to launch a new product within the same market, to launch or not to launch? Who are the target consumers? What are their demographics? Will this product be profitable? Who are the competitors? What type of sales and marketing capabilities are needed? VGS does a full analysis and provides answers to all the important questions, to provide our clients with a solid facts based advice on launching a new product into the market.

New Market Entrance Analysis

For our clients entering a completely new market, VGS provides an in depth analysis of the market; history, trends, and projections, in order to help them properly understand the market and make the right decisions.
Is there a sufficient demand ( demand ) for your market entrance product at this time ( timeliness ) in this place ( location ) ? What are the profitability margins in this market? Who are the major market shareholders and how much percentage of the market do they own? What is a realistic percentage of the market to target? What are the relevant government laws that guide the operators in the market? We provide an in depth analysis to guide our clients, and introduce them into a new market in the most intelligent way.

Mergers/Acquisitions

For your potential mergers and acquisitions, we provide a complete analysis to guide our clients through the decision making process. Why do you want to merge or acquire? What exact value are you seeking from the potential new merger? VGS will identify the key value adding competencies for our clients, and determine how to best harness it.
What are the financials to date of your target acquisition? What are its current operational capabilities? What is its current market share? What are the growth projections? What is the current value of its assets? What is the best valuation for this business? What will be the impact on the share price of our client ( if applicable )? How do you ensure a smooth transition? What are the alternatives to this acquisition/merger? We go in depth and provide the answers to these questions and more, giving our clients a detailed and comprehensive report to guide any potential mergers/acquisitions.

Manufacturing Processes Optimization

From value stream mapping/time studies, to identification of bottlenecks, to designing a more efficient process with increased productivity, we come in and add value to your manufacturing or production process, yielding a better output with higher efficiency. VGS makes use of six sigma tools and an analytical method tailored to your specific situation.
